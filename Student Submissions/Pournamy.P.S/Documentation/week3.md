In the third week, the instructor explained the milestone system and guided us on organizing the project in GitHub. We were instructed to create folders such as **Data** (with Raw Data and Processed Data), **Documentation** (weekly files from Week 1 to Week 8), **Power BI** (for later use), **Screenshots** (relevant images of outputs), and **README** (including title, problem statement, dataset description with rows and columns, key metrics, dashboard description, and tools used). As part of Milestone 1, we were given a task to clean a messy dataset containing fields like product category, price, sales, and profit margin, and prepare it for analysis. The instructor also briefly introduced Power BI data profiling features such as **Column Quality** (shows valid, empty, and error values), **Column Distribution** (displays the frequency and spread of values in a column), and **Column Profile** (provides detailed statistics about a column), and informed us that a presentation would be conducted on Friday including self-introduction, learning outcomes, and demonstration of our work.
