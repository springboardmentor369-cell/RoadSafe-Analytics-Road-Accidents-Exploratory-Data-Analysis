# Week 4 - Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

## Objective

The objective of this week was to explore the dataset and identify patterns, trends, and relationships within the data.

## Activities Performed

* Analyzed distribution of accidents and casualties
* Examined accident severity levels
* Studied patterns based on date and time
* Compared accidents across different vehicle types
* Identified trends and irregularities in the dataset

## Tools Used

* Power BI (Visualizations and Data View)

## Key Findings

* Higher number of accidents observed during peak traffic hours
* Certain vehicle types are more frequently involved in accidents
* Accident severity varies across different conditions

## Outcome

Gained meaningful insights from the dataset, which helped in designing the dashboard and selecting key performance indicators.
