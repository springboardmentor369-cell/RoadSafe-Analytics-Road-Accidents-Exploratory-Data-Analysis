# Week 5 - Question Framing from Dataset

## Objective

The objective of this week was to frame meaningful analytical questions from the dataset to guide data analysis and dashboard development.

## Activities Performed

* Analyzed dataset to identify key problem areas
* Formulated questions based on accident patterns and trends
* Focused on questions that can be answered using available data
* Selected relevant KPIs based on these questions

## Key Questions

1. What is the total number of accidents and casualties in the dataset?
2. Which type of vehicles are most frequently involved in accidents?
3. How does accident severity vary across different regions?
4. At what time of day do most accidents occur?
5. What is the trend of accidents over time (monthly or yearly)?

## Importance of Question Framing

* Helps in defining the scope of analysis
* Guides the selection of KPIs and visuals
* Improves clarity in dashboard design

## Outcome

Clear analytical questions were defined, which helped in building a focused and meaningful dashboard.
